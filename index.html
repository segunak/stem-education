About Me

Software Engineer & STEM Educator

I'm passionate about bridging the gap between technology education and practical application. As a Software Engineer at a company you might be familiar with, I bring real-world experience and a "explain it to me simply" attitude to my STEM education efforts.

Through interactive workshops, resources, and tools, my mission is to make STEM subjects accessible, engaging, and relevant to learners of all ages. I believe technology education should be both practical and inspiring!
