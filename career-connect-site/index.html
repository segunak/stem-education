Microsoft in Charlotte, North Carolina

Microsoft's Charlotte campus is a vibrant technology hub in the Southeast region. Our teams here work on a variety of exciting projects across cloud computing, AI, business applications, and more. The Charlotte office provides numerous opportunities for students and early career professionals interested in technology careers.

Discovery Day is just the beginning of your connection with Microsoft Charlotte. Stay in touch for future events, internships, and career opportunities in our growing tech community.
