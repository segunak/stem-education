Prompt Engineering with Microsoft Designer

Welcome! Follow the steps below to start your AI image generation journey. Make sure to read
and follow each step carefully!

  1. Use the sidebar menu to navigate to the first prompt.
  2. Click the "Generate Image" button to open Microsoft Designer.
  3. Fill in the blanks with your own adjectives or descriptive words to customize the AI-generated image. Do not leave the
    default words in place    , replace them with your own words to make your image unique!
  4. Each student should take turns filling in the blanks and generating an image for every prompt.
  5. Once all students have created an image for the prompt, discuss and compare your results.
  6. Repeat the process for each prompt until all five prompts are completed.

The image below demonstrates how to fill in the blanks on the Microsoft Designer create page. Replace the default words with your own creative descriptions, then click "Generate" to see your customized AI image.

Have fun exploring the power of AI and creativity with Microsoft Designer!
