Prompt Engineering with Microsoft Designer

Welcome! Follow the steps below to start your AI image generation journey. Make sure to read and follow each step carefully.

  1. Use the sidebar menu to navigate to the first prompt.
  2. Click the "Generate Image" button to open Microsoft Designer.
  3. Fill in the blanks with your own adjectives or descriptive words to customize the AI-generated image.
  4. Each student in the group should take turns filling in the blanks and generating an image for each prompt.
  5. Once all students have created an image for the prompt, discuss and compare your results.
  6. Repeat the process for each prompt until all five prompts are completed.

Have fun exploring the power of AI and creativity with Microsoft Designer!
