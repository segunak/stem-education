Apply to ALL Microsoft Internships

Apply to everything, even if the job title or description doesn't interest you. Internships are temporary and help you discover what you like. Microsoft covers all travel and housing costs for interns, so don't worry about the location. Apply everywhere, apply to everything. Not just at Microsoft, any internship you find on LinkedIn or Indeed, even if you've never heard of the company, even if the job title makes no sense, even if the description sounds AI generated, apply.

Apply to these active postings below. If they're closed, check Microsoft's internship search for new opportunities.

Pro tip: Set up job alerts on Microsoft careers to get notified when new internships open!

Connect & Message on LinkedIn

Connect with Segun Akinyemi on LinkedIn (the person behind this website), then message me saying you applied so I can process a referral for you. We're doing this through LinkedIn because you need to get comfortable using it professionally. It's essential for your career! Also, Microsoft owns it.

A referral is like a stamp on your application that says, "someone at Microsoft asked this kid to apply". It doesn't guarantee you'll get hired but it slightly increases the chance of your application being reviewed by a human and not auto-rejected by the AI filtering system.
